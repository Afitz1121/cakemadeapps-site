CapCheck HVAC

CapCheck HVAC is a capacitor diagnostic tool designed for HVAC technicians, electricians, and maintenance professionals.

Features

  • Single and dual capacitor evaluation
  • 50 Hz and 60 Hz support
  • Field measurement calculations
  • Custom tolerance input
  • Ad-free upgrade option

Availability

Available on Google Play.
